## Section Rationale

- Goal: forces one-task scope and avoids mixed-purpose guides.
- Prerequisites: prevents hidden assumptions.
- Related links: connects users to deeper rationale and exact reference data.
- Steps: gives deterministic execution path.
- Troubleshooting: reduces repeated support requests.
- Verify success: confirms outcome, not just command execution.
